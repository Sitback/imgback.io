Hate dealing with background images?

The Sitback image previewer will allow you to preview background images that you are going to use on your website in a variety of view modes, and at a variety of resolutions. It will give you a reasonable overview of how your image will look after implementation, and give you a guide as to how to create your images to keep the most important information on screen at all times.

Set a section height:
Changing the section height will give you a better idea of how your image will scale when used as a content background
Set background position:
Depending on the image in use, and the surrounding content, you can change the anchor point of the background image.

The default is centred both vertically and horizontally.

For more information please visit the MDN page for background-position
